Hi! I’m Fuyo Sugiyama.
For more than 40 years, she has been drawing many works using art, especially China paint (porcelain) and tole paint (oil, acrylic paint).
 
She has taught and learned from Okura Art China, which is unique to Japan, Royal Copenhagen, Hungarian Herend, French Nyon style, and Amerin style, and participates in conventions in the United States. In particular, she teaches many Japanese people and the wife of the embassy living in Japan, and spreads the joy of drawing as a hobby.
